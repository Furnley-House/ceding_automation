1. Problem Statement
Obtaining scheme and policy information from providers following Letters of Authority is currently slow, inconsistent, and heavily manual. Provider response methods vary widely and incomplete information is common, creating delays, rework, and risk of data errors.
The project covers all 10 stages of the ceding process:
Preparing the Letter of Authority (LOA)
Collecting client policy details and getting the LOA signed
Setting up ceding on Zoho CRM
Requesting information from the provider (via Origo or email / post)
Chasing the provider
Uploading documents and using AI to capture data
Handling missing information via call scripts and RingCentral / Palindrome transcription
Verifying the checklist
Notifying and sending to the adviser / paraplanner
Preparing recommendations

3. Project Deliverables
The project will deliver four things aligned to the 10-stage ceding process:
1. Ceding Application: An app that automatically reads provider documents, fills the checklist using AI, shows only the relevant fields for the specific plan type, flags missing information, generates call scripts, analyses RingCentral recordings via Palindrome to fill gaps, and produces a verified completed checklist for the adviser.
2. Revised Ceding Process: An updated LOA template with mandatory fields (provider name, address, plan type, policy number) and an updated advice standard requiring advisers to collect client policy details at the meeting (Stage 2). Note: the Zoho Blueprint checklist deliverable has been removed following confirmation from Matt that Blueprint checklist improvements are not practical as part of this project (Q-23).
3. Origo Workflow: Providers on Origo receive requests via URL link navigation rather than manual email or post.
4. Process Automation: AI data extraction, confidence-scored checklist, full audit trail, call script generation, Palindrome transcript analysis, and WorkDrive output -- eliminating current manual re-keying and reducing provider calls from 3-4 per case to 1.

5. Proposed Workflow -- 10-Stage Process
Each ceding case moves through 10 stages in sequence. The CA Team can navigate back to any previous stage to make corrections. Stage completion is tracked in a visible progress timeline on the case detail page.
Stage
Name
Primary Actor
Description
1
Prepare LOA
CA Team / Adviser
Create a Letter of Authority for the adviser to send to the client. The client will need to sign this, either online or on paper.
2
Collect Client Policy Details and Get LOA Signed
Adviser
At the client meeting, the adviser collects key policy details (provider name, address, plan type, policy number) manually or using Zoho Scan. Client to be told before the meeting to bring this information. Client signs the LOA -- some providers accept electronic signatures, others require a wet signature.
3
Set Up Ceding on Zoho CRM
CA Team
Create and configure the ceding case in Zoho CRM. Link case to client record and assign to CA Team.
4
Request Information from Provider
CA Team
If provider is on Origo: send electronic request via Origo portal (https://loa.unipass.co.uk/login). If not on Origo: send LOA and request form by email or post. Use post if a wet signature is required.
5
Chase the Provider
CA Team
If no response is received after 5 working days, follow up with the provider by phone or email.
6
Upload Documents and Use AI to Capture Data
CA Team
When the provider sends information: upload or scan documents into the system. The AI tool reads the documents and fills the checklist automatically. The CA team must check the information and edit anything incorrect. The system shows only the fields needed for the specific plan type. Accepted file types: PDF, Excel, Word, text.
7
Handle Missing Information
CA Team
If any information is missing: the system creates a call script to guide a provider call. The CA team calls the provider using RingCentral and records the call. The recording is passed to Palindrome for transcription and AI analysis to fill remaining gaps.
8
Verify Checklist
CA Team
Once all fields are complete: review and approve the checklist. Mark case as Ready for Review.
9
Notify and Send to Adviser / Paraplanner
CA Team
Notify the adviser and paraplanner that all information has been received. Assign a Zoho CRM task with a deep link to the case. Send the completed checklist for approval.
10
Prepare Recommendations
Adviser / Paraplanner
The adviser can now review the information, carry out analysis, and create recommendations. Paraplanner approves the checklist. Export to WorkDrive and trigger the Suitability Report process in Zoho CRM.
Re-review loop: if the Paraplanner or Adviser sends any field or the full case back for review at Stage 9 with a comment, the CA Team receives an in-app notification. The case status reverts to In Review. The CA Team must resolve the comment, make corrections, and re-mark as Ready for Review. A fresh approval cycle at Stage 9 is then required before the case can proceed to Stage 10.

7. Functional Requirements by Stage
Stage 1: Prepare LOA
Create a Letter of Authority for the adviser to send to the client.
LOA Status options: Not Sent / Sent / Signed (with date). LOA status must be confirmed before Stage 2 is accessible.
If provider is on Origo: show Open in Origo button linking to https://loa.unipass.co.uk/login.
If provider is NOT on Origo: show Send via Email button opening a pre-composed Outlook email via mailto link, with ceding department email pre-filled based on plan type prefix from the policy reference number, and the main provider email included as CC.
If wet signature required: show Print LOA button and display postal address from the Provider Directory.
All changes to case details are logged in the Audit Trail.
Stage 2: Collect Client Policy Details and Get LOA Signed
At the client meeting, the adviser collects key policy details: provider name, address, plan type, policy number.
Collection can be done manually or using Zoho Scan.
Client must be told before the meeting to have this information ready.
Client signs the LOA -- some providers accept electronic signatures, others require a wet signature.
LOA status must be recorded in the case before Stage 3 is accessible.
Stage 3: Set Up Ceding on Zoho CRM
Create and configure the ceding case in Zoho CRM. Link to client record and assign to CA Team member.
Stage 4: Request Information from Provider
Origo providers: send electronic request via Origo portal.
Non-Origo providers: send LOA and request form by email or post. Use post if wet signature required.
Provider contact details, routing method, and accepted signature type auto-populated from Provider Directory.
Stage 5: Chase the Provider
If no response received after 5 working days: follow up with provider by phone or email.
CA Team logs chase attempt in Audit Trail with date and method.
Stage 6: Upload Documents and Use AI to Capture Data
Drag-and-drop or click-to-upload. Accepted file types: PDF, Excel (.xlsx/.xls), Word (.docx/.doc), plain text (.txt), email-exported text.
Each document shown with: filename, file type icon, upload date, status (Uploaded / Processing / Extracted / Error).
Run AI Extraction button (or auto-trigger on upload). Progress bar with message: Extracting data from [filename] -- this may take up to 2 minutes.
Checklist fields update with extracted values, confidence scores (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW / MISSING), and source citations.
Split-panel view: document viewer opens alongside checklist. Each field source citation is clickable and scrolls the PDF panel to the exact page.
Conflict handling: if a new document contradicts an existing value, the field is flagged as CONFLICT and CA Team is asked to choose which value to keep.
Stage 7: Handle Missing Information -- Call Assist with AI Script
Missing fields summary grouped by category (Charges, Pension Details, Benefits, Scheme Information).
Auto-generated call script: opening greeting, provider phone number and department from Provider Directory, specific questions for each missing field, inline answer space.
Provider lookup card: main phone, main email, ceding department direct line and email, accepted LOA formats, Origo status, call notes.
Regenerate Script button if fields have changed.
Call Assist is not visible to Adviser and Paraplanner roles.
Stage 7 (cont.): Call Transcript with AI Assist
In production: call recording pulled from RingCentral via API, pushed to Palindrome, transcript returned automatically.
In prototype: Fetch from RingCentral button (stubbed with toast) plus manual paste as fallback.
Transcript input: paste Palindrome transcript into labelled text area or upload a .txt file.
Pre-defined expected questions panel (collapsible): lists questions that should have been asked, auto-generated from missing fields.
Analyse Transcript: AI matches answers to MISSING / LOW fields, pre-fills them, tags each with call source (date and CA user name).
Post-analysis summary: X fields filled from transcript. Y fields still missing.
Stage 8: Verify Checklist
Summary panel (pinned at top): count of HIGH confidence, MEDIUM/LOW, and MISSING fields. Progress bar. Updates in real time.
CA Team edits remaining fields manually. Auto-saves with 500ms debounce.
Mark as Ready for Review button enabled only when 0 MISSING fields remain.
All edits logged in Audit Trail with user name, timestamp, original AI value, and new value.
Stage 9: Notify and Send to Adviser / Paraplanner
Assign to Paraplanner modal: select user, add optional note. Case status updates to In Review.
In-app notification banner sent to paraplanner. Zoho CRM task created (stubbed). Deep link to case shown with copy-to-clipboard.
Checklist shown in clean read-focused layout with side-by-side PDF view.
Per-field: Approve, Request Review (routes back to CA Team with comment), Add Comment, Edit Value (logged).
Bulk checkbox selection for Approve Selected or Send Selected for Review with shared comment.
Approve All button (confirmation required). Request Full Re-review button (with comment).
Re-review loop: any field sent back triggers in-app notification to CA Team; fresh approval cycle required.
Once all fields approved: case status changes to Approved. Proceed to Export button activates.
Stage 10: Export and Upload to WorkDrive
Download Checklist: Excel (.xlsx) with Tab 1 (checklist: fields, values, confidence levels, source citations) and Tab 2 (audit trail). File naming: [ClientName]_[Provider]_[PlanRef]_[YYYYMMDD].xlsx.
Save to WorkDrive: button with toast. Folder path: Client / [ClientName] / Deal WIP. Case status updates to Complete on success.
Suitability Report summary card: key case facts (client, provider, plan type, transfer value, charges, key flags). Copy to Clipboard button.
Prepare Suitability Report button links out to Zoho CRM blueprint to trigger the suitability report process.
Audit Trail -- All Stages
Immutable log of every action. Cannot be deleted or edited by any role.
Log entry columns: Timestamp, User, Role, Action, Field, Old Value, New Value, Source.
Actions logged: case created, document uploaded, AI extraction run, field edited, conflict resolved, call script generated, transcript analysed, field approved, review requested, comment added, status changed, task assigned, case approved, checklist exported.
Display: vertical timeline with icons per action type. Filterable by action type and user.

11. Technical Requirements
11.1 Architecture
Application hosted on Furnley House Azure tenant using the organisation&apos;s own Azure OpenAI deployment.
No client or provider data to be sent to public OpenAI endpoints or external AI services.
Lovable AI (Anthropic API, claude-sonnet-4-20250514) used in prototype only. Production uses Azure-hosted OpenAI (confirmed by Nishant, 26 March).
Palindrome used exclusively for call audio transcription.
Three full-stack developers: Revathy S, Nishant, Srinath.
11.2 Integrations
Zoho CRM (TR-03, TR-04): Case auto-created via API when blueprint task reaches trigger stage (&apos;Request ceding&apos; in &apos;New Client, New Business&apos; blueprint). Pre-populated with client name, case ID, provider, plan type. Deep link from Zoho CRM task to app. Stubbed in prototype.
Zoho WorkDrive (TR-06): Checklist and audit trail exported as .xlsx and saved to WorkDrive. Folder: Client / Deal WIP. Naming: [ClientName]_[Provider]_[PlanRef]_[YYYYMMDD].xlsx. Stubbed in prototype.
RingCentral (TR-05): Call recordings retrieved via API and passed to Palindrome for transcription. Stubbed in prototype.
Origo (TR-12): URL link only to https://loa.unipass.co.uk/login. No API in MVP.
SSO (TR-02): Single Sign-On between Zoho CRM and the ceding app. Confirmed as feasible. Full implementation post-MVP.
11.3 Security and Compliance
All documents and extracted data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher) within the Furnley House Azure tenant.
GDPR compliance sign-off required from IT Manager / SLT before processing any real client data. HARD BLOCKER. Sign-off owner: John / Lee.
Data retention: 1 year backup for app and AI pipeline only (not for WorkDrive and Zoho CRM). Automated deletion after 1 year.
Role-based access control: CA Team = edit; Adviser / Paraplanner = approve and comment; Admin = manage directory and users.
11.4 Non-Functional Requirements
AI extraction completes within 2 minutes for standard documents up to 20 pages. Progress indicator shown for documents over 20 pages.
Page load under 3 seconds on 10Mbps+ connection. Tested from UK and Chennai.
99.5% uptime during UK business hours (08:00 to 18:00 GMT, Monday to Friday).
85% or more of fields at HIGH confidence across minimum 50 PDFs from 6+ providers before UAT clearance.
Adviser and Paraplanner complete review and approval tasks with no more than 10 minutes of training.
Supports concurrent use by up to 20 Chennai CA team users without performance degradation.

16. Glossary
Term
Definition
CA Team
Client Analyst Team based in Chennai. Responsible for processing ceding cases in the application.
CETV
Cash Equivalent Transfer Value. The transfer value offered for a Defined Benefit / Final Salary pension.
CRM
Customer Relationship Management system. Furnley House uses Zoho CRM.
DB
Defined Benefit pension scheme. Also referred to as Final Salary.
GIA
General Investment Account.
ISA
Individual Savings Account. Can be Stocks and Shares or Cash.
LOA
Letter of Authority. A document signed by the client authorising the adviser to obtain policy information from a provider.
MVP
Minimum Viable Product. The first production-ready version of the application.
Origo
A financial services data exchange platform used by many UK providers. LOA requests can be sent electronically via Origo.
Palindrome
Third-party call transcription service used to convert RingCentral call recordings into text transcripts for AI analysis.
RAG
Red / Amber / Green status indicator showing the health of a ceding case.
RingCentral
Cloud-based telephony platform used by the CA Team for provider calls.
SIPP
Self-Invested Personal Pension.
SSO
Single Sign-On. Allows users logged into Zoho CRM to be automatically authenticated in the ceding application.
UAT
User Acceptance Testing. The final testing phase before go-live.
WorkDrive
Zoho WorkDrive is the cloud storage and document management platform used by Furnley House.
